    Family unification is one of the main pillars of the immigration system in Canada. We understand how important is to have your family with you and will assist you in each and every step of the process. The sponsor must agree to financially support their family members and must sign a sponsorship agreement, which can range from a period of 3 years to 20 years, depending on the person being sponsored. We will assist you to understand the requirements of this undertaking and help you unite with your loved ones!
