A work permit allows a foreign worker to work in Canada. There are different types of work permits including post graduate work permit and the Spousal Open Work Permit.

In certain cases, employers in Canada need to obtain an LMIA (Labour Market Impact Assessment) from Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) in case they want to hire a foreign worker. In such a scenario, an employer would need a positive or a neutral LMIA which would verify that no Canadian worker is available to fill in the job offered to the foreign national. There are certain exemptions, please contact us to know further details.
