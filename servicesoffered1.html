Most visitors can stay in Canada for a period of 6 months at one time. We at Laurel Immigration would assist you and guide on the eligibility criteria for different reasons to visit Canada, be it visiting friends and family or for tourism. We would help you draft an invitation letter and gather all the necessary supporting documents.

Our services also include applications for super visa. Parents and Grandparents of eligible Canadian citizens and permanent residents can apply for a super visa which would allow them to stay in Canada for a period of up to 2 years at a time.
