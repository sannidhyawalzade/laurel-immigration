Know Us Better:

Laurel Immigration Services is a Canadian Immigration and Citizenship Firm offering high quality services in immigration matters. Located in Edmonton, we are approved by ICCRC (Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council). We work with clients all across the globe, offering personalised immigration solutions and evaluation.

We endeavour to provide you with the professional guidance and communication at every step of your application. As a current RCIC and a member in good standing with the ICCRC, we are up-to-date with the latest news, trends and opportunities to assist you to come and live in Canada.
