With plenty of options to chose from different colleges and universities; Canada offers a range of opportunities to international students arriving here. We can assist you to apply for a study permit, apply for a study permit extension or help you in changing institutions if you already studying in Canada. You may also be eligible to work while studying, which would help you to gain invaluable Canadian work experience. Once you graduate, you may also be eligible to apply for a Post Graduate work permit depending on your course of study.
